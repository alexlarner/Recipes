# Garlic Butter

## Ingredients

- 1 pound **butter**, unsalted
- 50 grams (1/2 cup) **parmesan**, grated (optional)
- 2 bulbs **garlic**
- 17 grams (1/4 cup) **oregano**, fresh, minced
    - Or 4 grams (4 teaspoons) **oregano**, dried
- 16 grams (1/4 cup) **basil**, fresh, minced
    - Or 4 grams (2 tablespoons) **basil**, dried
- Or 15 grams (1/4 cup) **parsley**, fresh, minced
    - 3 grams (2 tablespoons) **parsley**, dried
- 8 grams (1 tablespoon) **kosher salt**
- 2 grams (1 teaspoon) **black pepper**

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together until thoroughly combined
1. Let sit for at least overnight before using
