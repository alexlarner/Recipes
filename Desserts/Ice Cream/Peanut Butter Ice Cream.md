# Peanut Butter Ice Cream

Makes 1 1/2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 2 C **heavy cream**
- 2 C **milk**
- 1 C **brown sugar**
- 1 C **smooth peanut butter**

## Directions

1. Whisk ingredients together until sugar is dissolved
1. Pour into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
