# Beef Jerky

## Ingredients

- 1 pound **lean beef**, shaved/sliced thin
- 2/3 cup **soy sauce**
- 1/3 cup **fish sauce**
- 1/4 cup **fresh lime juice**
- 1/4 cup **coconut sugar**
- 1 bulb **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **galangal**
- 1 teaspoon **cayenne pepper**

## Instructions

1. Mix everything together and let marinate for 1-2 days
1. Place in dehydrator and let cook/dry at 160 for 4 hours or until the beef jerky cracks but does not break when you bend it
