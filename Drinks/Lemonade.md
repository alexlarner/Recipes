# Lemonade

Makes 2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 6 cups **water**
- 1 cup **fresh lemon juice**
- 1 cup **agave nectar**/**honey**

### Strawberry Lemonade

Add:

- 1/4 cup **strawberry juice** (optional)

### Blackberry Lemonade

Add:

- 6 oz blackberries, puréed

## Directions

1. If using fruit, first:
    1. Put the lemon juice and fruit together in a jar
    1. Puréed the mixture with an immersion blender
    1. Run the mixture through a fine mesh sieve to strain out the seeds
1. Mix all the ingredients together until well blended
